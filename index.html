About us

Computer vision lab of department of electronics and communication engineering of College of Engineering Trivandrum focus on research in the field of artificial intelligence, signal processing, computer vision, biomedical image processing, haptics, 3D reconstruction etc. In addition to academic research, computer vision lab offers internships and academic projects to UG and PG students of the department. There are 5 faculty members and 10 research scholars associated with the lab. Research outcomes of the lab are published in a number of peer reviewed journals as well as indexed conferences.

Research teams including UG students, PG students and faculty members participated in international challenges conducted as part of worldâ€™s top international conferences and were ranked in top positions. There were collaborative publications and funded projects with mechanical and civil departments. Participation in international challenges improves national and international visibility of the college and collaborative projects helps in widening the knowledge of students as well as faculty.
